Slide 1 — Problem & Solution (Title)
- Title: SwaraBharat — Civic Pulse
- Problem: Citizens face service gaps; officials lack real-time, anonymized signals.
- Solution: Lightweight AI that converts citizen messages into structured, anonymized insights for faster action.

Slide 2 — Tech & Safety
- Stack: Flask backend, simple AI engine (OpenAI/HF), lightweight frontend dashboard.
- Safety: Anonymized reports only; no PII persisted; rate limiting and fallback behavior.
- Demo endpoints: `/demo_examples`, `/demo_analyze`.

Slide 3 — Impact & Ask
- Impact: early detection of high-urgency issues, measureable improvement in response time.
- Metrics: #voices captured, % high-urgency flagged, time-to-resolution (proposal).
- Ask: mentorship, cloud credits for deployment, partner agencies for pilot.

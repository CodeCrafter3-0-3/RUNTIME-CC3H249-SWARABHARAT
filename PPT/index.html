Slide 2

Problem We Solve

Critical public issues are often discovered too late. The delay between citizen complaint and response creates avoidable escalation in health, water, and safety incidents.

Signal Fragmentation

Reports are scattered across calls, forms, and local messaging channels.

Manual Prioritization

Teams spend time sorting requests instead of resolving urgent cases.

Slow Escalation

Cross-team handoff is inconsistent, especially for location-based emergencies.

Slide 3

Platform Snapshot

SwaraBharat receives reports, classifies urgency and issue type, then routes action to dashboard operators, volunteers, and medical center administrators.

Frontend Surface

  • Citizen submission and multilingual reporting
  • Admin intelligence dashboard with map and analytics
  • NGO portal for volunteer task acceptance
  • Medical center panel for triage and dispatch

Backend Capabilities

  • Flask APIs with report lifecycle controls
  • AI analysis, semantic search, trend predictions
  • Priority explainability endpoint
  • Status updates and operational telemetry
Slide 4

Expanded AI Model Layer

The admin AI lab now supports multiple model options with safe fallback and direct model selection from the UI.

OpenAI Models

  • gpt-4o-mini
  • gpt-4.1-mini
  • Custom list via environment config

HuggingFace Models

  • bigscience/bloomz-1b1
  • google/flan-t5-base
  • mistral instruct option

Reliability

  • Model catalog endpoint
  • Selected-model analyze endpoint
  • Heuristic fallback when providers are offline
Slide 5

Medical Center Admin Panel

A dedicated medical operations interface enables faster triage for health incidents with dispatch controls and live load monitoring.

Core Actions

  • Filter by urgency and case status
  • Dispatch ambulance teams
  • Mark cases stabilized and resolved
  • Call emergency contacts and open map directions

Capacity Planning

  • Track ICU beds, ward beds, ambulances, trauma teams
  • Auto-computed load meter from critical pressure
  • Local persistence of capacity numbers per operator
